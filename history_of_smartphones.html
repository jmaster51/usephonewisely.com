According to bing, a smartphone is a mobile phone that performs many of the functions of a computer.

It usually has a touchscreen, Internet access, and an operating system capable of running downloaded applications.

The first smartphone which was referd to "Angler" at the time came out in 1992 (bing.com).

The Angler was developed by Canova while at IBM and demonstrated in November of 1992 at the COMDEX computer industry trade show.

In October of 2001 (according to pcmag.com) Samsung released the SPH-I300.

In 2007, Apple inc. released the Iphone. The average sales revenue of smartphones from 2013 to 2018 is around 426.2 billion dollars (keyideasinfotech.com).

Did you know that 80% of the world population use mobile devices and 42% of the US population use a Smartphone.

According to a survey by Compete, a web analytics firm, around 65% is using their Smartphones to read news feeds, post status updates, read & reply to messages and post photos.
